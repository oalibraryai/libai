TITLE: Khalifa University Library Services Overview

Khalifa University Library provides comprehensive academic library services to students, faculty, and staff at Khalifa University in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

LIBRARY BRANCHES:
Khalifa University Library operates two physical branches:
- Main Campus Library (SAN Campus, Abu Dhabi)
- Sas Al Nakhl Campus Library
Both branches provide study spaces, computer workstations, printing and scanning facilities, and access to physical collections.

BORROWING AND LOANS:
- Undergraduate students can borrow up to 10 items for 14 days
- Graduate students can borrow up to 15 items for 28 days
- Faculty can borrow up to 25 items for 90 days
- Most items can be renewed up to 3 times if not requested by another user
- Renewals can be done online through My Library Account: https://khalifa.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/discovery/account?vid=971KUOSTAR_INST:KU
- Overdue items may incur fines

STUDY ROOMS:
The library offers group study rooms that can be reserved online.
- Reserve a study room at: https://library.ku.ac.ae/rooms/
- Rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis
- Booking can be made in advance
- Group study rooms accommodate 4-8 people
- Individual study carrels are also available on a walk-in basis

INTERLIBRARY LOAN (ILL):
If a book or article is not available in the Khalifa University Library collection, you can request it through Interlibrary Loan.
- Submit ILL requests at: https://library.ku.ac.ae/ill/
- ILL is free for Khalifa University students and faculty
- Delivery time varies from 1-3 weeks depending on the source library
- Both physical books and electronic articles can be requested

PRINTING, SCANNING, AND PHOTOCOPYING:
- Printing and photocopying services are available in both library branches
- Black and white and color printing available
- Scanning is free of charge
- Students use their KU ID card for printing services

COMPUTER WORKSTATIONS:
- Desktop computers are available in both library branches
- All workstations have internet access and standard software
- Laptops can be borrowed for in-library use at the circulation desk

REFERENCE AND RESEARCH HELP:
- Librarians are available to help with research questions
- Research consultations can be scheduled
- Ask a Librarian service: https://library.ku.ac.ae/AskUs
- Library instruction sessions are available for classes

LIBRARY HOURS:
For current library hours, please visit: https://library.ku.ac.ae/hours
Hours may vary during exam periods, holidays, and Ramadan.
